The Kabyle Common Voice dataset does not currently collect explicit, structured accent labels. Participating speakers originate from diverse native tribal federations across *Tamurt n Leqbayel* (Greater and Lesser Kabylie) as well as global diaspora communities, which naturally injects rich, authentic phonetic variation into the audio corpus. Future metadata scheme upgrades will evaluate the introduction of self-reported regional or generational sub-accent markers to further granularize speech recognition training profiles.
