The Kabyle scripted speech corpus spans a broad variety of semantic domains to ensure automatic speech recognition (ASR) robustness:

* **General Conversation:** Everyday phrases, greetings, and common domestic expressions (v17.0-present).
* **Cultural Heritage:** Traditional vocabulary, regional proverbs, and historical references (v20.0-present).
* **Encyclopedic Prose:** Wikipedia-derived content covering global history, sciences, and geography (v17.0-present).
* **Modern Terminology:** Technology, digital media, administrative terms, and contemporary life vocabulary (v20.0-present).
